UC Berkeley's Hub for
all things Poker

Poker at Berkeley hosts a variety of initiatives such as monthly game nights, the UC Berkeley Poker DeCal, and of course, the annual Berkeley Poker Tournament!

Game Nights

We host weekly and monthly game nights for our members to do what they love - play poker!

Introduction to Poker DeCal

Poker at Berkeley runs the poker DeCal at Berkeley. We are on hiatus this semester, but will come back soon!

Berkeley Poker Tournaments

Every year, Poker at Berkeley hosts various Poker Tournaments with our sponsors. Make sure to fill out the membership form and join the discord to stay updated on upcoming tournaments!

Professional Development

We offer opportunities for our members to learn about applications of poker principles in the industry and offer various sessions with in-demand employers.
